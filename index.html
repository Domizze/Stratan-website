About us

STRATAN Inc is a full-service transportation company with headquarters in Las Vegas NV. We provide a variety of trucking services throughout the 48 states of the US. You can also find us on FMCSA, DOT, or Social Media. Our numbers and reviews speak for themselves. Specialized transport Services We at Stratan Inc specialize in transport, providing the best quality service in Opendeck, Stepdeck, Flatbed, and Heavy Haul. We handle the complete logistics so you will never have to worry about it, from picking up to delivering the goods.

History

STRATAN INC Trucking company was founded in 2017 by CEO Alexandr Stratan. Alexandr gave his last name to the company as a symbol of trust, confidence, transparency, and loyalty. Our Company never fails no matter if you work for us or if we work for you. Stratan Inc strives to deliver impeccable service. We will treat everyone with professionalism, commitment, and financial fairness.

Our Mission

Time today is priceless and, here at Stratan Inc, we definitely understand this. That is why we are trying to get our driver's downtime close to zero, by increasing their home time while still delivering our customer's goods on time. How do we do that?! By working in contract and maintaining good relations with our clients and with some of the top brands in the US for over ten years.  Our newer three years old fully warranty units are the key to our success. Stratan Inc buys mostly brand-new equipment to satisfy drivers' comfort and to keep everyone happy!
